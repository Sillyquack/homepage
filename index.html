Hi! I'm Robert Haugen.
I am taking on new projects!

I'm a Front End Web Developer with a passion for creating clean, responsive websites.

As a front-end developer, the variety of tasks and challenges at hand that are seemingly never-ending gets me going and wanting more. I often like to compare development to the craft/handcraft environment, as just like a carpenter, a brewer in a craft brewery, or an artist modeling their latest creation with their hands, the process between idea, crafting/coding, and the result is immediately recognizable and up for viewing and use. This gives me great pleasure. As soon as I finish a part of my ongoing project I can see it alive and kicking which gives developers a rare feeling of instant gratification for the work put in. Step for step. Although coding takes up much of my attention I keep my sanity and well-being in check by playing my favorite tunes on my drums, cuddling my dog of 13 years, and seeking out adventures in nature and the world in general with my wonderful fiance. Should life get boring between all the cuddling, adventuring, coding, and drumming I happen to be a micro-brewer and can whip up an excellent brown ale when days are dark and long or a Belgian wit in the bright summer days.

Can't wait to work with you!
